Hi, this is drumpads !

A drumpad is a clickable thing that when you press it, it plays a sound! There are pre-loaded samples to play and you can load your own by clicking Load... on the sample selector!

What means Hover Change Key?

If you are viewing this site on your PC, you can assign a letter to play determinated sample when you play that key. To do this, you just put the cursor (not clicking it!!!) on the pad you want to write a letter to it, and the letter will be assigned to that pad!

Once you know what the hovering thing is, by hovering the little dot besides thes pads, then you will be free to select a pre-loaded sample or load your own, possibilities are endless!
